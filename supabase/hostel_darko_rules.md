# Hostel Darko — Operations Rules
# Source: Excel DIRECTIONS tab (extracted for AI assistant context)

## Start of shift
- Check that phone and locker key are present
- Verify all deposits are accounted for
- Confirm all money collected by previous shift is correct

## Payments — CRITICAL RULES
- **DO NOT RECEIVE GUEST WITHOUT PAYMENT** — no exceptions, no matter what they say. First payment, then receive.
- Online payment via Booking.com is NOT possible — guests must pay on arrival by card or cash
- **DO NOT RECEIVE METAL COINS**
- If someone calls the business phone and speaks Serbian, send their number to the owner to call back

## Room policy
- Sometimes private rooms are rented as dorms when the hostel is full
- In that case, do NOT give guests the key to the entire room

## Guest check-in procedure
1. Welcome with a smile and ask about their journey
2. Greet and ask for their name
3. Request their passport — must be physical passport or ID, NOT a phone photo
4. Take a picture of the document, entry stamp, and if applicable, both sides of ID
5. Share the document photo in the group
6. Inquire about payment and city tax
7. Ask if they require a key
8. On payment slips for card transactions: take merchant copy, give customer copy to guest. Write guest name on piece of paper.
9. For key: request deposit — passport, ID, student ID, driver's license (avoid cash), or €10 / 1200 dinars. Write guest name on paper.
10. **Emphasize that the plastic key tag opens both the building entrance AND the hostel room doors** — show guest how to use it
11. Provide a tour: kitchen (after 11pm be quiet near rooms / preferably on terrace), bathrooms, terrace
12. No smoking (only outside or in the hallway by the window)
13. Escort guest to their bed
14. Make bed if necessary — ensure clean linens
15. Provide a set (linens) and a towel
16. Record passport details in the system

## End of shift handover
When finishing your shift, hand over to the next shift by ensuring:
1. Both bathrooms are clean
2. Kitchen is clean and tidied up
3. Terrace is clean
4. Any full garbage bags are emptied with new ones placed in their spots
5. Laundry should be hung to dry; any dry laundry should be folded and put away
6. If enough laundry for a full load, start the washing machine
7. Beds that were used must have dirty linens collected and beds made up properly
8. Inform the next shift about which guests have left, which have arrived, and where they are accommodated

## Cash register handover
- Hand over with a paper including: your full name, surname, and date
- On the paper: your name, date, list of who paid for what in cash, total cash amount
- Cannot officially hand over shift without completing all steps above
